    If heavy rain hits this corridor

    • The Addition Hills line (Lee St into A. Mabini) rides the high ground and skirts the basin, the drier call when rain is up.
    • Avoid the Nueve de Pebrero and inner San Juan side streets under a red warning, they pond fast.
    • Never cross knee-deep moving water; 15 cm of flow can stall a sedan.
    • Keep a go-bag: water, power bank, IDs, meds, flashlight.
